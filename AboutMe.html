I was Born and raised in Madison, Wisconsin. I graduated high school from La Follette in 2010 and went on to continue my work at the University of Wisconsin-Madison. Here, I earned a Bachelor of Arts in Anthropology and a minor in Archaeology. From there, I went on to Graduate School, also at the University of Wisconsin-Madison, and I am currently working to obtain my professional certificate in Geographic Information Systems. The next step after that will be earning a Master of Science in GIS and Web Map Programming.

I have gained a TON of experience in many different fields along the way! I worked in the field learning the ways of archaeology, and worked as a curatorial assistant learning the ways of caring for those precious unearthed artifacts. I even helped in completing a museum exhibit for the Village of Trempealeau, Wisconsin; some of my panels are available to view right here on this site! Check out my CV here.
